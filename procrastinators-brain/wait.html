Pattern 03 of 03

WAIT

Tools to lower the cost of starting — so perfect conditions stop being the prerequisite.

"I'll start once it's quieter. Once I feel ready. The right moment never arrives."
Why these tools? This pattern is rooted in perfectionism and nervous system threat response. Starting feels risky — what if you do it wrong? The brain avoids the discomfort of imperfection by waiting for ideal conditions. These tools lower the activation cost of beginning — making "just starting" structurally possible instead of emotionally heroic. Selected by Ahra Roh, OTR/L, ATP — not sponsored.
