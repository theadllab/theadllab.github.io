FREEZE

Tools to make your tasks visible — so your brain knows exactly where to start.

"I don't know where to begin. So I'll figure it out later. Later never comes."
Why these tools? When a task has no clear starting point, your brain can't form an action sequence. It enters a holding pattern — not laziness, but a genuine processing gap. These tools externalize the planning so your working memory doesn't have to hold it. Selected by Ahra Roh, OTR/L, ATP — not sponsored.
