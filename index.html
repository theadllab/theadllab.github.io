About

"Accessibility isn't a legal standard to meet. It's a design problem to solve."

I've spent 14 years as an occupational therapist watching patients — intelligent, capable people — struggle with products that were designed without them in mind. I've seen what works and what fails, not in focus groups, but in real homes, with real limitations.

That clinical depth is what I bring to product and environment consulting. If you're building for users with physical disabilities, I can tell you what your data can't.
