🫧 Pressure & Touch
Weighted Pressure
▾
  • Deep pressure activates proprioceptors → nervous system downregulates
  • Localized weight on chest or lap = same effect as full blanket
  • One of the most reliable calming inputs used in OT
Weighted Blanket
Full body · ~10% of body weight
→
Weighted Pillow / Cushion
Localized · lighter commitment
→
Sleep Socks
▾
  • Warm feet trigger vasodilation — one of the body's first sleep-onset signals
  • Foot warmth → core body temp drops → sleep condition met
  • Simple, passive, evidence-backed
Fluffy Sleep Socks
Soft · no compression
→
Compression Sleep Socks
