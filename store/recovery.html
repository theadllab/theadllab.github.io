Recovery

Your nervous system doesn't wind down on command. For sensory-sensitive brains, the transition from "on" to "off" is its own task — and the wrong environment makes it nearly impossible.

These tools are selected to reduce the sensory load that keeps your system activated, so the shift toward rest can actually happen.
