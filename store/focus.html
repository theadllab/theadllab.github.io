Category 03 — Already Done For You
Focus &
Stimulation

Stillness isn't neutral for every brain. For some nervous systems, the absence of input makes focus harder — not easier. The brain keeps searching for stimulation, and that search is what pulls you away from work.

These tools add the right kind of input — tactile, movement, auditory, oral — so your nervous system has something to settle into instead of something to look for.
