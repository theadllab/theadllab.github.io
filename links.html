About this space

Sensory processing isn't just for children.

Every human nervous system takes in, filters, and responds to sensory information — from birth until the end of life. You're doing it right now. Most people just don't have a language for it.

ADL Lab exists for adults who have always felt a quiet mismatch — with environments, routines, relationships, or work — but never had a framework to name it. No diagnosis required. No labels needed.

This isn't clinical OT. It's occupational science and neuroscience — applied to the texture of everyday adult life. The goal isn't to fix you. It's to understand the fit between you and the world around you.

Neuroscience-based Occupational science No diagnosis needed For all adults Disability-inclusive

Which pattern are you?

Low Registration

Needs more input to notice. Appears calm or disengaged.

Sensation Seeking

Craves stimulation. Adds movement, noise, novelty.

Sensory Sensitive

Notices everything. Easily distracted by the environment.

Sensation Avoiding

Actively limits input. Needs control of the environment.
